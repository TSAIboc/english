Step 2: 英文轉寫

Online about possible changes proposed by nominees for President-elect Trump's cabinet. One of those changes will be to tap water. Sherald Mercedes explains why the proposal is already causing some backlash.

Why has the nomination of Robert F. Kennedy Jr. as the head of Health and Human Services sparked a new debate about fluoride in drinking water? RFK Jr has said he will advise local water systems to remove fluoride from public drinking water.

According to the Centers for Disease Control, close to 63% of Americans have fluoridated water coming out of their taps. Local municipalities make the decision whether or not to add fluoride to the water supply. The US Environmental Protection Agency only mandates a limit on how much can be put in.

So what is fluoride? It is a naturally occurring mineral that is already found in some water supplies. That's actually how we discovered it helped teeth. Dentists noticed that people living in areas with fluoride in the water had less tooth decay and better enamel. In 1945, Grand Rapids, Michigan became the first city in the world to add fluoride to its water supply.

Research shows the rate of cavities in children there decreased by more than 60%. The debate over fluoride stems from what happens when there is too much in the water. A recent scientific paper found a possible link between high fluoride levels and lower IQs in children, but those fluoride levels were double what is recommended by the EPA. Experts say more research is needed and warn that facts, not conspiracy theories, should inform that debate.

Step 3: 中文翻譯

關於川普當選總統的內閣提名人提出的可能變化的線上討論。其中一項改變關乎自來水。謝拉爾德·梅賽德斯解釋了為何這一提議已經引發了一些反對的聲音。

為何羅伯特·F·甘迺迪（小）被提名為衛生與公共服務部部長人選會引發了一場關於飲用水中氟化物的新辯論？小甘迺迪表示，他將建議地方供水系統去除公共飲用水中的氟化物。

據美國疾病管制與預防中心稱，近63%的美國人使用的自來水是加過氟的。地方市政當局決定是否要在供水系統中添加氟化物。美國環保署僅對添加量的上限作出規定。

那麼，氟化物是什麼呢？它是一種自然產生的礦物質，已存在於一些供水系統中。這其實是我們發現它對牙齒有益的方式。牙醫注意到，生活在水中含有氟化物地區的人蛀牙較少，牙釉質較好。1945年，密西根州的大急流城成為世界上第一個在供水系統中添加氟化物的城市。研究顯示，當地兒童的蛀牙率下降了60%以上。

關於氟化物的爭論源自於水中氟化物含量過高會造成什麼影響。最近的一篇科學論文發現，氟化物水平過高可能與兒童智商較低有關，但這些氟化物水平是環保署建議水平的兩倍。專家表示，需要進行更多研究，並警告說，應該用事實而非陰謀論來指導這場辯論。
